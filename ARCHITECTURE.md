# ARK-CORE Architecture

---

## 🧠 System Overview

ARK-CORE is structured as a layered governance system:

1. Entropy Layer  
   - Raw input, randomness, anomaly, unknown signals  

2. Signal Layer  
   - Structure detection  
   - Signal vs Noise separation  
   - Entropy validation  

3. Governance Layer  
   - Decision protocols  
   - Conflict resolution  
   - Policy enforcement  

---

## 🔁 Core Flow

Entropy → Signal Authentication → Governance Decision

---

## 📦 Modules

- SIGNAL_AUTH_v1.yaml  
  → Detects structured signals from entropy  

- RESOLVER_API.json  
  → Handles conflict resolution  

- ARK-MANIFESTO.yaml  
  → Defines core governance principles  

---

## ⚖️ Design Principles

- Heterogeneity > Uniformity  
- Potential > Immediate Utility  
- Reality > Efficiency
