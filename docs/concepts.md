# Core Concepts

## Heterogeneous Intelligence
ARK-CORE assumes that intelligence should not converge into a single dominant form. Diversity in reasoning strategies, representations, and perspectives is treated as a system-level asset.

## Signal vs Noise
The framework distinguishes between meaningful signal and compressive noise. However, it avoids prematurely discarding weak or minority signals, as they may carry future value.

## Entropy Governance
Instead of minimizing entropy blindly, ARK-CORE regulates entropy. Structured entropy is allowed and preserved when it contributes to long-term adaptability.

## Non-functional Asset Preservation
Not all valuable system components are immediately useful. ARK-CORE preserves non-functional or dormant structures as potential future resources.

## Observer Relativity
System evaluation depends on the observer. ARK-CORE avoids enforcing a single global perspective, allowing multiple evaluation frames to coexist.
